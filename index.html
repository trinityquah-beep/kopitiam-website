Our Story

Three generations of coffee brewing tradition

Since 1985 in the Heart of Klang

Kopitiam Rasa was founded by Mr. Lim Ah Kow, who brought his family's secret coffee recipe from Penang to Klang. What started as a small stall has grown into a beloved institution where generations of Klang residents have gathered.

We still use the traditional sock filter method to brew our coffee, paired with charcoal-toasted bread and homemade kaya (coconut jam). Every cup tells a story of Malaysian heritage.
